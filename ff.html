Marketing Intern

Fresno Filmworks
May 2014 – September 2014
Fresno, CA

I was the summer marketing intern for Fresno Filmworks, a local organization that shows independent films that aren't shown in Fresno theaters. I helped write film synopses and create promotional materials for the organization's monthly film screenings. I assisted the Fresno Filmworks President with brainstorming activities for screenings, researching the upcoming films for blog posts and advertisments, and any organizational tasks that needed to be done.

  • • Compiled research and produced content for website and social media.
  • Developed, pitched, wrote, and produced story ideas for advertising on flyers, radio, local news stations.
  • Made promotional videos.
  • Assisted with and created activities for events.
